# CepPOS got 3rd place at 2021 of IDC Financial Services
![Alt text](/src/assets/news/images/x.webp)

CepPOS mobile application wins 3rd place of Digital Channel Projects at 2021 IDC Financial Services.

The CepPOS, as we define it: a super-easy & hyper-functional mobile POS get 3rd place from international competition. CepPOS has succeeded to be an awardee product from the first version and just after a few months of launching.

CepPOS is the name of the mobile application that converts smartphones to a fully functional Point Of Sale device. It has been developed by EticSoft for Emlak Katılım Bank.
