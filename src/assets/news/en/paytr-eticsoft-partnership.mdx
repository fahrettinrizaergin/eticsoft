# PayTr & EticSoft Signed Partnership for SanalPos PRO!
![Alt text](/src/assets/news/images/paytr-partnership.jpg)

EticSoft and PayTr have joined forces to enhance digital payment solutions. This strategic partnership will integrate PayTr's virtual POS infrastructure with EticSoft's SanalPos PRO platform, offering merchants a more comprehensive payment processing experience.

The collaboration aims to provide businesses with advanced payment features while ensuring secure and seamless transactions for their customers.
